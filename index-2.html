Welcome to Keke's Days

A few things that make this yours:

  1. Swipe left or right on a day's card to move through the week.
  2. Tap any task to edit its time, name, or details.
  3. Drag the handle on the left of a task to reorder it.
  4. Tap the note under the day title to write today's context.
  5. Pick a color theme up top — it's saved just for you.
  6. Turn on Reminders to get a nudge before each task.
